AI Consulting & Automation FAQ

Everything you need to know about evoAI, our services, and how we work.

🤖 // General

evoAI is an AI consultancy and product studio that helps ambitious enterprises integrate, scale, and future-proof their technology using artificial intelligence. We're a lean, focused team with a genuine commitment to our clients' success — working as partners, not just vendors.

Alongside our consultancy work, we're also building an AI-native product launching in 2028. Stay tuned.

Our headquarters are in Canada. However, we work with clients globally — across the UK, EU, US, and APAC regions. We operate remotely and engage with clients wherever they are.

We primarily work with ambitious enterprises and scale-ups looking to integrate AI meaningfully into their operations. That said, if you're a smaller business with serious AI ambitions, we'd love to have a conversation — reach out and let's see if we're a good fit.

We work across a range of industries including finance, logistics, healthcare technology, retail, professional services, and SaaS. AI strategy and intelligent automation are broadly applicable — if you have data and processes, we can likely help.

  • Intelligent Automation — replacing repetitive workflows with smart AI agents that learn, adapt, and operate autonomously.
  • Predictive Analytics — turning your data into accurate forecasts for demand, churn, pricing, and risk.
