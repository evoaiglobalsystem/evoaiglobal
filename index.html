// Under the hood

Technical capabilities

The core technologies we apply to solve real business problems kept lean, practical, and focused on what actually delivers value.

Core AI
LLM Integration
Embed OpenAI or Anthropic models into your products e.g. an internal chatbot that answers policy questions or drafts emails in your brand voice.
RAG & Knowledge Retrieval
Let AI search and reason over your own documents e.g. a sales assistant that pulls answers from your entire proposal library in seconds.
Automation
AI Agent Development
Autonomous agents that complete multi-step tasks e.g. an agent that monitors inboxes, categorises requests, and triggers the right workflow automatically.
Document Intelligence
Extract and structure data from unstructured files e.g. reading supplier invoices or contracts and routing key fields into your system without manual entry.
Workflow Automation
Connect AI decisions into your existing tools e.g. auto-assigning support tickets based on content, urgency, and team availability.
Data
Predictive Modelling
Forecast what's likely to happen next e.g. which customers are likely to churn, which products will sell out, or where demand will spike next quarter.
Data Pipeline Design
Clean, connect, and move your data reliably e.g. unifying sales, ops, and finance data into one place so AI models always have accurate inputs.
Deployment
Cloud Deployment
Ship AI solutions on AWS, Azure, or GCP e.g. hosting a customer-facing AI feature that scales automatically with demand at minimal cost.
API Integration
Connect AI capabilities to the tools you already use e.g. plugging a forecasting model directly into your ERP, CRM, or dashboard with no disruption.
